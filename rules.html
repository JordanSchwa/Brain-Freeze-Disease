Rules for Brain Freeze Disease

Code of Conduct

1) An acceptable Canteen must specialize in selling frozen desserts. An acceptable Canteen may sell other things besides frozen desserts, but may not be selling all things a pirate needs. (no super-canteens)

2) Cool Whip does not count as an acceptable dessert alone, but may be used in addition to enhance a true dessert.

3) The price per canteen for all pirates in question may not exceed 10 monies. Several pirates may pool their bounty to buy a more expensive treasure to share, but individually may not contribute more than 10 monies.

4) A Captain`s Log will be kept throughout the voyage in which Pirates will record the date and location at the top, then list the desserts and approval rating of Pirates present.

5) Jaywalking is allowed and required.

6) Magical Portals (phone) may only be used to determine optimal directions and locations and pictures. Contact through social media, video watching sites, or MEMES is prohibited. Pirates are required to be present so that they are able to board a ship at moments notice.

7) Mutinies are prohibited and failure to comply to the Code of Conduct will result in plank walking and banishment from the Brain Freeze Disease.
