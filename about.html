About Brain Freeze Disease

We are a group of high school seniors in Redwood City who wanted to live out their childhood dream. We plan to go to every ice cream or cold dessert place within a 10 mile radius of our homebase and record our experience at each over the course of our last year in this place.

We wanted to take the opportunities we had to explore the Bay Area further and keep connected through our summer and last year together. And what better way to do that than with ice cream!
